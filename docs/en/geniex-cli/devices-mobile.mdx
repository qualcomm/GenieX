## Mobile Devices

GenieX supports on-device inference on Snapdragon 8-series mobile platforms running Android.

### Supported Chipsets
- Snapdragon 8 Elite
- Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5
- Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 6

### Get Started
You can either use a Snapdragon Elite-series mobile devices or click "Compute" at [Qualcomm Deveveloper Cloud - Mobile page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/mobile) and open a `Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen5` device.
