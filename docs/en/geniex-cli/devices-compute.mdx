## Compute Devices

GenieX runs on Snapdragon X-series compute platforms, enabling local AI inference on Windows on Arm laptops and desktops.

### Supported Chipsets
- Snapdragon X Elite
- Snapdragon X2 Elite

### Get Started
You can either use a Snapdragon X-series laptop or click "Compute" at [Qualcomm Deveveloper Cloud - Compute page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/compute) and open a `Snapdragon X Elite` or `Snapdragon X2 Elite` device.
