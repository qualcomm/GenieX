## IoT Devices

GenieX supports Qualcomm IoT platforms for edge AI inference in smart cameras, robotics, industrial automation, and embedded systems.

### Supported Chipsets
- Qualcomm QCS9075
- Qualcomm QCS9100
- Qualcomm QCS8275

### Get Started
You can either use a Snapdragon X-series laptop or click "Compute" at [Qualcomm Deveveloper Cloud - IOT page](https://qdc.qualcomm.com/devices/compute) 
and open a `Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9075` or `Qualcomm Dragonwing IQ-9100 Beta` device.
